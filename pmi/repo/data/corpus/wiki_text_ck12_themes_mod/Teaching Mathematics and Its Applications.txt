Teaching Mathematics and Its Applications is a triannual peer-reviewed academic journal in the field of mathematics education. The journal was established in 1982 and is published by Oxford University Press on behalf of the Institute of Mathematics and its Applications. The editors-in-chief are Duncan Lawson (Newman University, Birmingham), Chris Sangwin (Loughborough University, and Anne Watson University of Oxford. The journal is abstracted and indexed in the British Education Index, Education Research Abstracts, Educational Management Abstracts, Educational Technology Abstracts, MathEduc Database, and ProQuest databases.
