Energy in Lithuania describes energy and electricity production, consumption and import in the Republic of Lithuania. Energy policy of Lithuania will describe the politics of Lithuania related to energy more in detail. Lithuania is net energy importer. Primary energy use in Lithuania was 98 TWh and 29 TWh per million persons in 2009. Oil companies include Klaipdos Nafta and ORLEN Lietuva. Natural gas companies include Lietuvos Dujos.
