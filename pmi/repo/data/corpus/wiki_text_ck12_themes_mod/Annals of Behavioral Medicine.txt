The Annals of Behavioral Medicine is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal published by Springer Science+Business Media on behalf of the Society of Behavioral Medicine. It covers research in behavioral medicine, both basic and applied and publishes empirical studies as well as reviews. The editor-in-chief is Christopher France (Ohio University). It is the official journal of the Society of Behavioral Medicine and was established in 1979.
