"Scotch gray" redirects here. Foor the chicken breed, see Scots Grey. For the cavalry unit, see Royal Scots Greys. Eudonia murana, the Scotch gray or wall grey, is a moth of the Crambidae family. It is found in most of Europe. The wingspan is 1823 mm. Adults are on wing from June to August, possibly in two generations. The larvae feed on various mosses growing on rocks and walls, including Hypnum cupressiformis, Dicranum scoparium, Bryum capillare and Grimmia pulvinata.
