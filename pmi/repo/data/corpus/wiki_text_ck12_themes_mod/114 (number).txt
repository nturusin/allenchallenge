114 (one hundred [and] fourteen) is the natural number following 113 and preceding 115. 114 is an abundant number, a sphenic number and a Harshad number. It is the sum of the first four hyperfactorials, including H(0). At 114, the Mertens function sets a new low of -6, a record that stands until 197. There is no answer to the equation (x) = 114, making 114 a nontotient. 114 appears in the Padovan sequence, preceded by the terms 49, 65, 86 (it is the sum of the first two of these). 114 is a repdigit in base 7 (222).
