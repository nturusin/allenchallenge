The Joint parliamentary meeting on the Future of Europe is a meeting of parliamentarians from the European Parliament and from the national parliaments of the member states of the European Union on the topic of the future of the European Union. The first of these meetings occurred on 2006-05-08/2006-05-09; the second occurred on 2006-12-04/2006-12-05. The third took place in Brussels on 11 and 12 June 2007.
