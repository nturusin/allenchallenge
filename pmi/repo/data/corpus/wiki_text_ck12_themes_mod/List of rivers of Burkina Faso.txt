This is a list of streams and rivers in Burkina Faso. Burkina Faso has three main rivers the Black Volta, the Red Volta and the White Volta. Areas near rivers are affected by tsetse flies and simulium flies. This list is arranged by drainage basin, with respective tributaries indented under each larger stream's name.
