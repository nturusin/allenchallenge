The Miller Medal is an award of the Royal Society of Canada given for outstanding research in any branch of the earth sciences.The award consists of a gold-plated silver medal and is awarded every two years if there is a suitable candidate. In 1941, twelve friends of Willet Green Miller, FRSC (18671925), a distinguished geologist, and a guiding force in the development of the Ontario mining industry, subscribed funds to provide the Willet G.
