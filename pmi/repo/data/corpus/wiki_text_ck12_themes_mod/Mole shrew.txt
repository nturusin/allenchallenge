A mole shrew is a shrew that resembles a mole. Species with that name include: Two species in the genus Surdisorex, native to Kenya: Aberdare Mole Shrew (Surdisorex norae) Mt. Kenya Mole Shrew (Surdisorex polulus) Four species in the genus Anourosorex, native to Asia: Assam Mole Shrew (Anourosorex assamensis) Giant Mole Shrew (Anourosorex schmidi) Chinese Mole Shrew (Anourosorex squamipes). Taiwanese Mole Shrew (Anourosorex yamashinai)
