Howard Ensign Simmons, Jr. (June 17, 1929 April 26, 1997) was an American chemist who discovered the SimmonsSmith reaction. He was born on June 17, 1929. In 1952, Simmons received the Charles Goodyear Medal. In 1976, Simmons served as Chair of the Organic division of the American Chemical Society. He died on April 26, 1997.
