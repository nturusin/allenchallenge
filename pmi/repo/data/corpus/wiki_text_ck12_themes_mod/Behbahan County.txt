Behbahan County (Persian: ) is a county in Khuzestan Province in Iran. The capital of the county is Behbahan. At the 2012 census, the county's population was 279,703 in 49,607 families. The county is subdivided into three districts: the Central District, Tashan District, and Zeydun District. The county has three cities: Behbahan, Tashan District, Sardasht.
