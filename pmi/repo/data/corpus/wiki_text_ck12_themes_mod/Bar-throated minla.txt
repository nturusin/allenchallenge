The bar-throated minla or chestnut-tailed minla (Minla strigula), is a species of bird in the family Leiothrichidae. Traditionally, it has been placed in the genus Minla. It is found in the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia, ranging across Bhutan, India, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand, Tibet and Vietnam. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical moist montane forests.
