Noether is the family name of several mathematicians, and the name given to some of their mathematical contributions : Max Noether (18441921), father of Emmy and Fritz Noether, and discoverer of : Noether inequality Max Noether's theorem Emmy Noether (18821935), professor at the University of Gttingen and at Bryn Mawr College Noether's theorem (or Noether's first theorem) Noether's second theorem Noether normalization lemma Nther crater, on the far side of the moon, named after Emmy Noether Fritz Noether (18841941), professor at the University of Tomsk Gottfried E. Noether (19151991), son of Fritz Noether, statistician at the University of Connecticut
