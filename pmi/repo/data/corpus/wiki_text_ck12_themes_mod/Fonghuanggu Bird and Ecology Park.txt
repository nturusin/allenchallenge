The Fonghuanggu Bird and Ecology Park (Chinese: ; pinyin: Fnghungg Nio Yun Shngti Yunq) is a bird park in Lugu Township, Nantou County, Taiwan. The park is located at an altitude of 650-850 meters above sea level and covers an area of 32 hectares.
