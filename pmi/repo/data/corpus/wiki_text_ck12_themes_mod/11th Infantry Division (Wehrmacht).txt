The 11th Infantry Division (11. Infanterie-Division) was a formation of the German Wehrmacht during World War II. Formed 1 October 1934 as Infanteriefhrer I in Allenstein it was renamed 11. Infanterie-Division on 15. October 1935 with the disclosure of German rearmament. The division was evacuated from Courland pocket on 30 April 1945.
