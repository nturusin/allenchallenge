The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to sustainability: Sustainability capacity to endure. For humans, sustainability is the long-term maintenance of well being, which has environmental, economic, and social dimensions, and encompasses the concept of stewardship and responsible resource management. Sustainability Sustainabiity is divided into two main branches: sustainability science and sustainability governance.
