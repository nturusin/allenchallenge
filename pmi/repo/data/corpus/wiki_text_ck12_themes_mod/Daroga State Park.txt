Daroga State Park is a 90-acre (36 ha) Washington state park located on the Columbia River 8 miles (13 km) north of Orondo along the edge of Washington's Channeled Scablands. The park has 1.5 miles (2.4 km) of river shoreline and offers picnicking, camping, boating, fishing, swimming, waterskiing, birdwatching, wildlife viewing, and other athletic facilities.
