Arizona has been the name of three ships of the United States Navy. USS Arizona (1858), laid down in 1858 and served in the American Civil War. USS Arizona (1869), a steam frigate launched in 1865 as the USS Neshaminy. She was renamed twice, carrying the name Arizona for less than three months. USS Arizona (BB-39), a Pennsylvania-class battleship launched in 1915 and sunk by Japanese bombers in the attack on Pearl Harbor 7 December 1941.
