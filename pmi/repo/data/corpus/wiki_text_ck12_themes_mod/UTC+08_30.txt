UTC+08:30 is an identifier for a time offset from UTC of +08:30. This time is used in: UTC+08:30 was used for Changpai Time Zone in the Republic of China. UTC+08:30 was Standard Time of South Korea from 1954 to 1961. On 15 August 2015, North Korea replaced Korea Standard Time by adopting Pyongyang Time, which is UTC+08:30.
