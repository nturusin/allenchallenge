James "Jim" Wiegold (15 April 1934 4 August 2009) was a Welsh mathematician. Born in Trecenydd, Caerphilly, he earned a Ph.D. at the University of Manchester, England in 1958, studying under Bernhard Neumann, and is most notable for his contributions to group theory. Wiegold died from leukaemia on 4 August 2009, in Penarth, Vale of Glamorgan.
