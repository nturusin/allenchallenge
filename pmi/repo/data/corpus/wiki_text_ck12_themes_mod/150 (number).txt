150 (one hundred [and] fifty) is the natural number following 149 and preceding 151. 150 is the sum of eight consecutive primes (7 + 11 + 13 + 17 + 19 + 23 + 29 + 31). Given 150, the Mertens function returns 0. The sum of Euler's totient function (x) over the first twenty-two integers is 150. 150 is a Harshad number and an abundant number.
