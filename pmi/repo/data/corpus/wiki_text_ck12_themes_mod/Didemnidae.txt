Didemnidae is a family of colonial tunicates in the order Enterogona. These marine animals are found in shallow water on the seabed. Members of this family have small zooids that form encrusting colonies. The body of each zooid is divided into a thorax and an abdomen. The World Register of Marine Species lists the following genera: Atriolum Kott, 1983 Clitella Kott, 2001 Coelocormus Herdman, 1886 Didemnum Savigny, 1816 Diplosoma Macdonald, 1859 Leptoclinides Bjerkan, 1905 Lissoclinum Verrill, 1871 Polysyncraton Nott, 1891 Trididemnum Della Valle, 1881
