175 (one hundred [and] seventy-five) is the natural number following 174 and preceding 176. 175 is an odd number, a composite number, and a deficient number. It is a decagonal number, a 19-gonal number, and a centered 29-gonal number. 175 is an Ulam number, and a Zuckerman number. It is the magic constant of the nn normal magic square and n-Queens Problem for n = 7.
